How to play
  1. Each player starts in their own corner.
  2. Your first piece must cover that corner square.
  3. Every piece you place must touch one of your own pieces corner-to-corner — but never side-to-side.
  4. You can touch an opponent's pieces on any side — just not your own.
  5. When no player can place any more pieces, the game ends automatically.
  6. Fewest squares left unplaced wins (lowest score wins).
Keyboard shortcuts
  • R — rotate piece 90° clockwise
  • F — flip piece left ↔ right
